Cheesy Turkey & Veggie Pasta Bake

A comforting, make-ahead dish pasta bake that's easy to reheat and toddler-friendly.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 3 cups whole wheat pasta (penne or rotini)
  • 3 cups marinara sauce (no raw onion, mild)
  • 2 cups baby spinach (fresh or frozen, chopped small)
  • 1 cup shredded zucchini or carrots
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella (reserve 1/2 cup for toping)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until just al dente (slightly firm). Drain.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil. Brown turkey with garlic & onion powder.
  3. Stir in marinara, spinach, zucchini/carrots. Simmer 5 min.
  4. Combine pasta, turkey sauce, and 1 cup mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Top with remaining mozjarella + Parmesan.
  6. Bake at 375– F for 20 min until bubbly and golden.

Batch/Storage Tips

  • Cool quick; portion into sealed containers.
  • Fridge: up to 3 days
  • Reheat: microwaive or oven until 165° F.
  • Freezes well up to 2 months.
  • Toddler tip: chop spinach fine; shred turkey small; serve in fork size bets.
