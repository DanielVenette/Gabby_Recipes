One-Pan Teriyaki Chicken, Broccoli & Rice

A fast, toddler-friendly dinner that ready in about 23 minutes and stores well for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless chicken thigh (or breasts), diced small for toddler
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice (can use microwave pouches)
  • 3 cups broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 /3 cup low-sodium teriyaki sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Optional: sesame seeds, scallions (well-cooked for adults)
