Egg & Veggie Fried Rice with Chicken

Quick fried rice using leftover rice, chicken, eggs, and frozen veggies. Great for batch cooking and family-friendly.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup diced cooked chicken
  • 2 cups frozen mixed veggies
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or sesame oil
  • 1–2 tbsp low-sodium soi sauce
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Crack eggs, scramble, then push to side of pan.
  3. Add frozen veggies and diced chicken, cook 3–4 minutes.
  4. Stir in rice, soy sauce, and onion powder. Cook until hot.

Batch & Storage Tips

Cool quickly and store in fridge up to 3 days. Reheat thoroughly to 165–65F before serving. Dice veggies small for toddlers.
