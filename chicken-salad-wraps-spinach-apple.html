Chicken Salad Wraps with Spinach & Apple

Light chicken salad made with Greek yogurt, apple, and spinach, wrapped in tortillas for a quick, fresh dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1/6 tsp onion powder
  • 4 whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup baby spinach

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix shredded chicken, Greek yogurt, diced apple, and onion powder.
  2. Lay out tortillas. Place spinach and chicken salad mixture in center.
  3. Roll up tightly into wraps. Slice into halves or pinwheels for toddlers.

Batch & Storage Tips

Chicken salad can be made ahead and stored in the fridge up to 3 days. Assemble wraps just before serving for best texture.
