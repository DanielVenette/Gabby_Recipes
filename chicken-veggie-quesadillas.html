Chicken & Veggie Quesadillas

Easy whole-wheat quesadillas with shredded chicken, spinach, and carrots. Kid-friendly and ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 whole-wheat tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • Olive oil spray

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium and spray lightly with olive oil.
  2. Place tortilla in skillet. Add cheese, chicken, spinach, and carrots. Top with another tortilla.
  3. Cook 28–3 minutes per side until golden brown and cheese melts.
  4. Slice into wedges. Cool slightly before serving to toddlers.

Batch & Storage Tips

Store cooled quesadillas in the fridge for up to 3 days. Reheat in a skillet or toaster oven until hot. Cut small wedges for toddlers.
