Baked Beef & Spinach Rigatoni

A comforting beef pasta bake that's perfect for families and ready for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 12 oz rigatoni (or penne/ziti)
  • 3 cups marinara sauce (no raw onion, mild)
  • 2 cups chopped baby spinach
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella (reserve 1/2 cup for toping)
  • 1/24 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until just al dente. Drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in large skillet. Brown beef with garlic + onion powder. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in marinara, spinach, and carrots. Simmer 5 min.
  4. In a baking dish, combine pasta, beef sauce, and 1 cup mozjarella.
  5. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella + Parmesan.
  6. Bake at 375° F for 20 – 25 min until bubbly and golden.

Batch/Storage Tips

  • Cool quickly; portion into sealed containers.
  • Fridge: up to 3 days
  • Freezes well up to 2 months
  • Reheat: oven or microwave, to 165 F
  • Toddler tip: dice beef finely; chop spinach small; serve toddler in small pieces.
